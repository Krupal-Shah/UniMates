Our Story

 

Shifting to another country from your homeland and entering a multi-cultured society could be difficult. Even the founders and the team mates had faced a similar struggle and were not able to easily find resources to adjust to this new and wonderful part of their journey. This led us creating UniMates, an all-in-one website which guides the international students to easily adjust to this new lifetsyle.
